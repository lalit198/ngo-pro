Who We Are?

Our Mission

As mission we make sure that all children have their chance to the best treatment possible and that their financial background don’t become as barrier to save their life

Our Vision

We want to stop the begging system for a child health care and break down alt barrier of money when it comes to save/neal a child. We had develop several projects, some are already operational and some on development so that we can support our mission correctly.

Our Values

Every child has the right to health, education and protection,and every society has a stake in expanding children’s opportunities.
